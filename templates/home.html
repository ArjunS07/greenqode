Educate about your greenery

Easily add printable QR codes to trees and plants in your community to help people learn about its biodiversity.

More meaningfully engage your community with your greenery


Every park, residential complex, school or public area wants their community to learn about the wide variety of trees and plants that are present there.

GreenScan makes it easy for community managers to help community members to learn all about these trees and plants surrounding them.

Here's what happens:

A community manager can...

Add community trees and plants to their dashboard

Obtain automatically generated QR codes for each item they add

Print an autogenerated sheet of QR codes for all of their trees

Place the QR code cutouts throughout their community



... and then a community member can:

Scan the QR code on a tree or plant using their mobile phone camera

Automatically has a webpage open on their phone

Read the latest information entered by the community manager for that item

It's all customisable

As a community manager, every detail that you and the community see about your greenery is customisable.

The name of the plant or tree - the scientific one, common one, or both!

A description of the plant or tree. We've added a Google button so it's easier for you to find information

A list of the other places in the community where you can find the same trees or plants.

The quantity of the item or clusters of the item. This determines how many QR codes are printed.
